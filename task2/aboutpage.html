ABOUT COMPANY

Ducati is an Italian motorcycle manufacturing company headquartered in Bologna, Italy. The company is directly owned by Italian automotive manufacturer Lamborghini, whose German parent company is Audi, itself owned by the Volkswagen Group. Wikipedia Owner: Volkswagen Group Parent organizations: Lamborghini, Audi, Volkswagen Group Founders: Antonio Cavalieri Ducati, Adriano Cavalieri Ducati, Marcello Cavalieri Ducati, Bruno Cavalieri Ducati Founded: 4 July 1926, Bologna, Italy Headquarters: Borgo Panigale, Bologna, Italy

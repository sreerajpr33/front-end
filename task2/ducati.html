Ducati Panigale V4 is a super bike available in 3 variants and 3 colours. The Ducati Panigale V4 is powered by 1103cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 212.5 bhp and a torque of 123.6 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, Ducati Panigale V4 comes up with anti-locking braking system.
